Chorizo Street Tacos

These chorizo tacos use just five ingredients and taste amazing!

Ingredients

    One chorizo sausage link, casing removed and meat crumbled
    Two tablespoons chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
    Four corn tortillas
    Two tablespoons of chopped onion, or to taste
    Two tablespoons of chopped cilantro, or to taste optional

Directions

    1) Combine crumbled chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce in a bowl
    2) Heat a skillet over medium-high heat; add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp, five to seven minutes. Transfer to a plate, reserving grease in the skillet
    3) Heat tortillas in reserved grease in the skillet over medium heat until warmed, one to two minutes per side. Stack two tortillas for each taco, then fill with corizo, onion, and cilantro
